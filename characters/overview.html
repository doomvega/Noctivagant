Original Characters

All of my little guys...

Many artists have a place to store all their characters, whether that's in a document, a notebook, or a site like Toyhouse. I realized that I didn't have something like that, so here we are! A little directory of all my characters, for reference and safekeeping.

Each of their names is a hyperlink to their own pages.

Unless you'd like to cycle through each page one-by-one?

HEAVENSPEAK

Ribbons: perhaps the most recognizable of the bunch, Ribbons appears on all my livestreams and is in charge of leading a Heavenly revolution off-camera.

Radiation: Ribbons's closest friend, Radiation tags along wherever Ribbons goes. He's constantly trapped in this battle of "what is legal" and "what is moral" during Ribbons's revolution ordeal.

Lyre: quiet and observant. Lyre barely speaks above a whisper and appreciates guidance from Timekeep and the rest of her friends.

Timekeep: strong, but very quiet like Lyre. When it speaks, it speaks for a reason. It has a dry, well-aged sense of humor, like a fine wine (but would it approve of that joke? I literally invented it, I should know!).

Pokémon

Symi: my Pokésona (still up for debate). Just a Simisage who hangs out in Pinwheel Forest.

Basil: a Grass-type Samurott. Curious yet fierce. His tail is a big leaf!

Elwen: a Lilligant-Delphox (Lillifox) fusion. They like to dance and garden.

Fursonas & Furries

Fursona: my oldest fursona who no longer has a name. They're never seen without their scarf.

Borealis: an Arctic fox who was granted the beauty of the Aurora Borealis. He struggles with complicated emotions at times.

Jupiter: a space wizard with a cool staff and wings. Jupiter is all about astronomy and knows the secrets of the stars.

Dungeons & Dragons

Lorcán: a Foxfolk pirate who's lost his way.
