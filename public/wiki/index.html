Welcome to the Noctivagant Wiki. This is where I keep information about my projects, stories, and characters.

HEAVENSPEAK

  • Ribbons - the most recognizable of the HEAVENSPEAK cast, Ribbons is in charge of leading a Heavenly revolution.
  • Radiation - Ribbons's closest friend, Radiation tags along wherever Ribbons goes. He struggles with morality during the revolution.
  • Timekeep - wise and reserved, Timekeep prefers to look at things logically than emotionally.
  • Lyre - quiet and observant. Lyre barely speaks above a whisper and appreciates guidance from Timekeep and the rest of her friends.

Pokémon

  • Symi - just a Simisage who hangs out in Pinwheel Forest.
  • Basil - a Grass-type Samurott. Curious yet fierce.
  • Elwen - a Lilligant-Delphox (Lillifox) fusion. They are a forest guardian.

Fursonas
