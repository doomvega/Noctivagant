Socials

We have a Discord server! If you're interested in what I do, want to meet other folks in the community, or just need a space to hang out and meet other creative folks, hop in. You can also opt-in to my stream and video notifications, so you'll never miss when I go live or upload a new clip.

Streaming

My main presence on the Internet right now is through my livestreams! I stream all sorts of things, from games to art to programming and more. Stream schedules get posted to my socials and to my Discord server every week.

  • Twitch: where all the livestreams happen. Twitch is the best place to catch me if I'm live.
  • MakerTube: an alternative to YouTube, and another place to catch my streams. MakerTube chat is integrated with OBS, so no need to be on two websites at once to interact with the stream!
  • YouTube: beyond VOD storage, my YouTube channel is also home to clips, Shorts, livestreams, and community posts.
  • Instagram: clips and such get uploaded here.
  • TikTok: clips and such get uploaded here.
