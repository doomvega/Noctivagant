About DOOMVEGA

Howdy! As I said before, I'm DOOMVEGA. Most people call me Vega, but I have three names total, the other two being Atlas and Jupi, short for Jupiter. I am a transmasc and queer multi-media artist making cool things on the internet. I don't like to make my identity the center of what I do, but it certainly plays a part in that.

So, what can you call me?

Whichever of my three names you want! Atlas was the first name I picked out when I realized I was trans, with Vega coming after I played DOOM for the very first time, and Jupiter following soon after. I almost took Mars, but that seemed antithetical to Vega.

I don't really mind how you refer to me pronouns-wise, just mix it up (this includes neos)! I don't use she/her, though. Ribbons does!

What do I do?

I make a little bit of everything. Generally, I make...

  • Music
  • Digital art
  • Videos
  • Video games
  • Fanfiction & original writing
  • Small programs
  • Websites just like this one!

...and I'm always trying new things! I dabble in sewing, pin-making, and bullet journalling.
