Twitch

some important information regarding my Twitch channel.

I wanted a space to go more in-depth with the rules around my Twitch channel, and explain stuff like ads.

Please do not sub, give me bits, or donate to my Twitch channel if you are in a tight financial situation. I don't want to cause strain on anyone. Subs, bits, donations, and other forms of monetary support are never, ever required but deeply appreciated.

All prices here are in USD. They are not in PokeDollars. :]

Rules:

  1. My streams are for 18+ (ADULT) audiences. The mature tag on my streams is there for a reason. That reason being, I have absolutely zero desire to interact with random children or high schoolers. If you are found breaking this rule, you will be instantly banned. (Specifically, if you are underage and you follow me, that is grounds for banning.)
  2. Bigotry will not be tolerated. Racism, queerphobia, etc. is NOT cool. Firstly, don't waste my time. Secondly, I will not stand for bigotry of any kind. Your hatred has no place here. You will be banned and reported. It's 2024. We should not have to have this conversation.
  3. Don't be weird towards your streamer. I'm here to have fun. Think before you speak. I'm not going to be an expert at everything. I play games and make art for fun, and I do not appreciate folks coming in specifically to harass, compare, or judge me and/or my abilities.
  4. Do not backseat. It ruins the experience for everyone. If you are unaware of what backseating is, this is a great (short) article on backseating. Basically, if I'm playing a game, I want to experience it for myself. I don't need help from the audience, no matter how well-meaning you may be.
  5. If you were banned, it was likely for good reason. Bans are permanent and final. This is self-explanitory. Break any of these rules and you will be banned.
  6. Have fun! I know I'm a bit of a hardass with all these rules but I promise that 99% of the time, I'm a chill guy. Just don't be a jackass or shitstirrer on my streams, and we can all have a good time.

Advertisements:

Yes, I run three minutes of ads on my streams every hour. This allows me to make a little bit of money doing something I love. I don't enjoy running ads, so here is what you can do when the ads hit.

Get up and stay away from your machine. When the ads start rolling, I will drop whatever I'm doing and cut to BRB. You should take this time to get up, get yourself a snack or some water, and move around. The stream will (likely) be here when you get back.

Subscribers do not have to watch ads. You get access to some cool emotes, and it helps keep me afloat!

DO NOT: mute the stream, close, or minimize the tab. You can mute the tab, but muting the stream directly, for whatever reason, does not count as a view!

When I get out of the situation I'm in, I plan to take a small portion of each paycheck to turn into gift subs. If and when the stream revenue picks up, that will go right back to the community, too.
