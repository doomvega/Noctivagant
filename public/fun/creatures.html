In my travels across the Internet, I have come across some awesome creatures and creature-keeping games that I'd like to share.

Dopcom

Dopcom Creechers is a new petsite emulating the feel of Neopets. Users hatch Creechers from eggs, give them magical charms and take care of them, all in hopes of raising and releasing an exceptionally powerful magical pet.

Each pet listed below can be interacted with if you have an account on Dopcom. I highly recommend joining!

Tamanotchi

Tamanotchi is like Tamagotchi on the Web! All of these pets can be interacted with without an account. Help my Tamanotchi grow and escape their cases!
