Oh my goodness. Where do I begin with RTVS?

I found RTVS through HLVR:AI back in March of 2020, soon after it released on YouTube. I fell in love instantly, and I will admit that for a while I thought there was indeed artificial intelligence involved. I adored how Wayne and the crew bounced off each other, I loved their humor, I loved the way they told a story— I couldn't help but eagerly dive into whatever else Wayne and the crew had done. When I worked for a certain retailer I would watch the AI Dungeon VODs on my lunch breaks because not only were they HLVR:AI adjacent, but they were really funny. From there, I just kept exploring. I came to really admire the crew. Everyone is fantastically talented in a myriad of different ways. For example, Holly has an incredible voice range and Log is an incredibly talented artist. They're responsible for some of the funniest things on the internet, like that Moonbase Alpha video and Who Woulge, among many others.

Long story short— I deeply admire the RTVS crew and love what they do.
